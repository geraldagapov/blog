# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
This is a personal blog hosted on GitHub Pages, designed to share insights on reading materials and thoughts regarding engineering practices and management within big tech environments. The posting schedule is targeted for a monthly cadence.

## Target Audience
The primary audience for this blog consists of:
- Software Engineers
- Engineering Managers

## Key Features
- **Categorized Posts:** Content will be organized with tags such as "Engineering" and "Management" for easy navigation.
- **Comments Section:** A space for readers to engage in discussions and share their perspectives.
- **Newsletter Subscription:** A mechanism for readers to subscribe and receive updates on new content.

## Tone and Voice
The blog aims to strike a balance between several tones:
- **Professional and Authoritative:** Establishing credibility in the field.
- **Conversational and Personal:** Maintaining a relatable and accessible connection with the reader.
- **Academic and Research-oriented:** providing depth and rigor to the insights shared.

## Success Metrics
Success will be measured by:
- Sparking meaningful discussions in the comments section.
- Establishing thought leadership within the engineering community.
