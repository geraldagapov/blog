# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise:** Writing should be direct and to the point, favoring clarity and brevity to effectively communicate technical concepts and insights.

## Visual Identity
- **Minimalist:** The design should be clean and uncluttered, prioritizing the readability of the text and the content itself.

## Code Formatting
- **Syntax Highlighting:** All code snippets must utilize appropriate syntax highlighting for the specific programming language to enhance readability.

## Navigation & Links
- **External Links:** Links to external resources should open in a new tab to ensure the reader maintains their place on the blog.

## Accessibility
- **No Specific Constraints:** There are currently no strict constraints or specific requirements defined for accessibility.
